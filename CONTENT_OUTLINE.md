## FAQ Answers

### What type of projects do you handle?
We handle software, hardware, cloud, mobile, website, embedded systems, IoT, and edge AI projects. We can support early prototypes, MVPs, business platforms, dashboards, connected devices, and production-ready systems.

### Do you work with startups?
Yes. We work with startups that need help turning an idea into a prototype, MVP, or scalable product.

### Can you build both hardware and software?
Yes. Vortiq Dynamics can support hardware, PCB design, firmware, cloud platforms, APIs, dashboards, mobile apps, and web applications.

### Do you provide maintenance after delivery?
Yes. We can provide support, updates, improvements, monitoring, and ongoing technical maintenance after launch.

### Can you develop custom IoT and embedded systems?
Yes. We can design custom IoT and embedded systems involving sensors, microcontrollers, firmware, connectivity, cloud dashboards, and edge intelligence.
